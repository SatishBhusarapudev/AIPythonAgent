Research on US Population:

The United States is currently the third most populous country in the world with an estimated population of 340,110,988 as of July 1, 2024, according to the U.S. Census Bureau. This represents a 2.6% increase from the 2020 federal census count of 331,449,281 residents.

The US population figures include all 50 states and Washington D.C. The country continues to grow through both natural increase (births minus deaths) and international migration, maintaining its position as the most populous nation in the Americas and the Western Hemisphere.
